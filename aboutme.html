About me

My name is Dominik Cario, I am 24 years old and come from Vienna, Austria. I am a video game enthusiast and am therefore pursuing a comprehensive career as a video game developer. In my free time, I work on some personal project, which involve a lot of programming, databases, networks and other parts of IT. I also have practical experience as a video editor and in graphic design, so I am also active in these areas.

Videogames

I have been interested in video games since I was a child. Not only the modern ones, but also the classics, especially Gameboy and Wii, are my favorites. Indie games in particular inspire me greatly.

3D - Modeling

I started using Blender at the end of November 2023 and have been trying to improve my skills ever since. It all started with a simple cube and has now evolved into a cartoon-style rendering of Mario Bros.

Programming

I started learning HTML at technical college and have been hooked ever since. Therefore, I am aiming to obtain a degree in programming in the languages C# and JavaScript, as well as in the markup languages HTML and CSS and the scripting language SQL. This also allowed me to gain experience with tools such as WPF, Unity, and SQL Management Studio.

Video and image editing

Starting with Movie Maker and Gimp, I now work with Adobe products. I have already been able to use these skills in commercial areas. For example: Graphics for a Pokémon Go news team or for short videos for the creator Techtastisch.

Modding

I'm a big fan of taking games and consoles apart and installing new parts. Whether it's small things or completely new things.

HTL Mödling

Starting in 2016, I graduated from the HTL Mödling technical college in electronics in 2021 and the advanced course in computer science in 2025 with the Matura. I am particularly interested in programming, as I am passionate about video game development.
I was able to learn a lot through numerous projects, and our main focus there was C#, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL.
